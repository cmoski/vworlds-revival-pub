To log on to the world you must:

  1. Enter your nickname and password. A nickname must be from 3 to 12 characters and cannot contain spaces. A nickname can contain letters and numbers. Remember to write these down so you don't forget!
  2. Type in your profile information (optional).
  3. Check any information that you would like to make private (optional). Checking information as private means only people that you have selected to be your friends can see this information.
  4. Click Go button to enter the world and wait for the application to appear.
