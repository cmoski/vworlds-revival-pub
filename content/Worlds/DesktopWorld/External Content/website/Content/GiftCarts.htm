Gift Carts
You can select any of the items on the Gift Carts in the 3D view and send these to another member or send them to yourself. You can send up to six items at time.
  • Flower Cart: Click a flower to create a bouquet of flowers! Choose from daffodils, irises, roses and tulips.
  • Balloon Cart: Click a balloon to send to a friend! There are many shapes to choose from!
