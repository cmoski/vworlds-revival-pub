Desktop World Overview
Desktop World provides demonstrations of integrated web pages, auto-navigation through clicking on destinations and customizable avatars.

The Desktop World Overview gives you a bird's eye view of Desktop World. Click the labels to see more information about those locations.

The Resource Desk has information about the Virtual Worlds Group organization and platform.

The Seattle Desk has a Seattle Guide with a listing of places to live, restaurants and fun things to do.

The Discussion Area is a service which displays discussions about member-created topics. You reply to a topic or create a new one of your own.

The Gift Carts area is where you can go to send gifts to other members or yourself. Your can wear a gift you've received as well!

The Game Area has suggestions for games to play in Desktop World and links to games on the web.

The Music Garden is a beautiful place with musical sculptures. Try clicking on parts of the sculpture to hear the musical tones. Great place to relax and make music with other members!
