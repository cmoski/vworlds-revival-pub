  • Fun Things to Do:
    • Seattle Art Museum The collections of the Seattle Art Museum number approximately 23,000 objects representing a wide range of art-from ancient Egyptian reliefs to contemporary American installations using photography and video. The collections are particularly strong in five areas: Asian, African, Northwest Coast Native American, modern art and European painting, and decorative arts.
    • Seattle Aquarium excels at telling the stories of the Puget Sound and its sealife. The Puget Sound exhibit starts at a replica of a Northwest beach and then leads underwater.
    • Seattle Center
    • Pacific Science Center and Planetarium offers science exhibits which include the new tropical butterfly house and fascinating animal exhibits. Check out what Boeing IMAX Theater is currently showing at the Pacific Science Center.
