Locating Others in Desktop World

To locate another member in Desktop World, click the People tab. From the menu that appears, click the Who Is Here option to the list of members currently visiting Desktop World. Click the Who Was Here option to view a list of people who are members of Desktop World, but are not currently logged on.


The Who Is Here and Who Was Here submenus list, collectively, all of the members of Desktop World whether they are currently in Desktop World or not. When you click the name of a member from either list, several options appear below that person's name, including access to his or her profile information.

To move closer to another member currently visiting Desktop World, use the Approach option. The Approach option is located under that member's name on the Who Is Here list. When you click the Approach option, your character automatically navigates to the person you are looking for.

Note The Who Is Here and Who Was Here lists do not automatically show updated information while you view these lists. To see whether a member has logged on or off while you are viewing the People menu, re-click the People menu, and the information will be refreshed.



To find another member in the People menu
  1. Click the People tab .

    The Who Is Here section appears by default.

  2. To view previous members not currently in Desktop World, click Who Was Here.

To approach another member in Desktop World
  1. Click the People tab .

    The People menu appears, and the Who Is Here submenu is opened by default.

  2. From the Who Is Here submenu, click the name of the member you are looking for.

  3. From the list of that appears below his or her name, click Approach.

    In the 3-D view, your character moves to the person you want to approach.

    Tip You can also Approach another member by right-clicking that person and choosing Approach from the menu that appears.
