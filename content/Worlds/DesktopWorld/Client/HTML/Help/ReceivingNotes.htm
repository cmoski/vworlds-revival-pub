Receiving Notes

In Desktop World, you can receive notes from other members at any time. A flashing exclamation point on the My Things tab indicates you have received a note. In the chat history area, a View button with a message next to it that says, "You have a new Note from [the sender]," also appears.

Received notes are stored in the My Notes area on the My Things menu. A closed envelope appears beside each unread note. When you read a note, an open envelope is displayed. From this menu, you can delete a note by clicking the note and choosing the Throw Away option from the list of choices that appears below the note.

Note If you have placed another member on your My Ignored list, you will not receive notes sent by that person.

To read a note that has been sent to you
  1. Click the My Things tab .

  2. From the My Things menu, click My Notes.

  3. Click the note you want to read.

    The note appears in the Web pane.

    Tip You can also read a note that has been sent to you by clicking View next to the message in the chat history box that says, "You have a new Note from [the sender]."
