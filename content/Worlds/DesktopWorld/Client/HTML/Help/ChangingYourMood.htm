Changing Your Mood

In Desktop World, you can communicate your mood to others by choosing a mood in the My Appearance section of the My Profile menu.


Selecting a mood is a good way to let others know without words how you are feeling. A mood is displayed above your shoulder and is visible to all others in Desktop World. There are four moods to choose from, or you can choose to show no mood by selecting None (which is the default). Your mood is not saved between sessions in Desktop World.

To select a mood
  1. Click the My Profile tab .

    The My Appearance section appears by default.

  2. Click the menu next to the Mood label.

  3. Select a mood from the list.

    Your selection appears in the Preview area.

  4. To keep your selection, click .

Note You may also change your mood by right-clicking your character in the 3-D pane and clicking My Profile from the menu that appears.
