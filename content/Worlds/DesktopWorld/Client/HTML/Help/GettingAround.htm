Getting Around in Desktop World
In Desktop World, you can navigate your character throughout the 3-D section of the main window to explore the world around you. There are three ways to move your character:
  • Use the Destinations menu navigation buttons, such as Resource Desk.
  • Use the mouse in the 3-D view.
  • Use the keyboard.

To move with the navigation buttons
The navigation buttons in the Destinations menu are similar to an "autopilot" for moving to specific areas of Desktop World quickly. When you click an area on the list, your character is automatically moved there for you, and you can see this movement in the 3-D view.
  1. Click the Destinations tab .

    A list of Desktop World locations, such as the Game Room, appears in the legend.

  2. Click a location on the list.

    Your character is transported to the new location.


To move with the mouse
Using the mouse to navigate in the 3-D pane can be challenging at first, but it can be fun once you get the hang of it.
  1. Click inside the 3-D viewing area.
  2. Hold down the left mouse button and move the pointer in the direction you would like to go.

      



To move with the keyboard
Some people find they are more comfortable using the keyboard arrow keys to move around. In addition to the arrow keys, there are several other keys that enable you to get a better view of the world around you. The PAGE UP, PAGE DOWN, and HOME keys allow you to look up, look down, and reset to your normal view, respectively.

Important To use the keyboard to move around in the 3-D space, you must first click inside that space.

Keyboard KeyAction
LEFT ARROWYour character turns to the left.
RIGHT ARROWYour character turns to the right.
UP ARROWYour character moves forward.
DOWN ARROWYour character moves backward.
PAGE UPYour character looks up.
PAGE DOWNYour character looks down.
HOMEYour view is reset to normal.
