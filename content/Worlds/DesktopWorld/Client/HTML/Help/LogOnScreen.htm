Log On Screen

To log on to Desktop World you will need to pick which world to enter, then provide your nickname and password, and choose either male or female.

  1. Enter your nickname and password. A nickname must be from 3 to 12 characters and cannot contain spaces. A nickname can contain letters and numbers. Remember to write these down so you don't forget!
  2. Type in your profile information (optional).
  3. Check any information that you would like to make private. Checking information as private means only people that you have selected to be your friends can see this information.
  4. Click Go button to enter Desktop World and wait for the application to appear.
