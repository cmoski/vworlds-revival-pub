Selecting Your Nickname

When you first enter Desktop World, you choose a nickname to identify yourself in the Log On to Desktop World box. The name you select does not have to be your real name, but you can include your real name in the My Details section of the My Profile menu.



Guidelines for nicknames
  • A nickname must be from 3 to 12 characters.

  • A nickname cannot contain spaces.

  • A nickname can contain letters and numbers.

  • Your nickname must be different from nicknames of other visitors. If the name you choose is the same as another visitor, Desktop World adds a number to your name to distinguish you from other people. For example, if you typed the nickname "John" and there was already a member of Desktop World named "John," your nickname in Desktop World would be "John1."


To select a nickname
  1. In the Log On to Desktop World box, type your nickname in the Name box.

  2. Click Go.
