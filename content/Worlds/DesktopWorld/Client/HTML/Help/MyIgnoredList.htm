My Ignored
The My Ignored area displays the members of Desktop World that you have prevented from communicating with you. Members on your My Ignored list have been notified that they have been added to this list. This list is saved between sessions in Desktop World.




My Ignored options
Profile
Displays the profile information of members whom you are ignoring. Profile details are displayed in the Web pane by default, but all information is the color gray, indicating that you are ignoring that person.


Make Friend
Sends a message to an ignored member that informs them that you are removing them from your My Ignored list and asking for that member's permission to add them to your My Friends list.


Undo Ignore
Removes a Desktop World member from your My Ignored list, allowing that member to resume communicating with you. If you remove a member from this list, a message appears in that person's chat history area that says, "[Your name] is no longer ignoring you." A similar message appears in your chat history area to confirm that you are no longer ignoring that member.


Whisper To
Sends a private, whispered message to a person you are ignoring. A member on your ignored list cannot respond to your message until you remove them from that list.


Send Note
Sends a private, personalized note to a member on your My Ignored list. That member cannot respond until you remove them from your My Ignored list.


Approach
Moves your character in the 3-D view to the current location of the member you are ignoring.
