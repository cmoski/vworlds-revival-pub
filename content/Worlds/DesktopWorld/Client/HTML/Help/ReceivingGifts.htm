Receiving gifts

In Desktop World, you can receive gifts from other members at any time. A flashing exclamation point on the My Things tab indicates you have received a gift. In the chat history area, a View button with a message next to it that says, "You have a new Gift from [the sender]," also appears.

Received gifts are stored in the My Gifts area on the My Things menu. A closed package appears beside each unopened gift. When you open a gift, an open package replaces the closed package. From this menu, you can delete a gift by choosing the Throw Away option from the list of choices that appears below each opened gift.

When you receive a gift, you can display it in the 3-D view by having your character "hold" the gift in its hand. Other members will be able to see the gift your character is holding, and if your camera position is set to facing-you, you can see your gift in the 3-D view. To hold a gift, click Hold from the list of options below each opened gift. To stop holding the gift, click Put Away. You can hold only one gift at a time.

Note If you have placed another member on your My Ignored list, you will not receive gifts sent by that person.



To view a gift that has been sent to you
  1. Click the My Things tab .

  2. From the My Things menu, click My Gifts.

  3. Click the gift you want to view.

    The gift appears in the Web pane.

    Tip You can also view a gift that has been sent to you by clicking View next to the message in the chat history area that says, "You have a new Gift from [the sender]."



To hold a gift in the 3-D view
  1. Click the My Things tab .

  2. From the My Things menu, click My Gifts.

  3. Click the gift you want to hold.

    The gift appears in the Web pane, and a list of options appears below the gift in the My Things menu.

  4. From the list of options, click Hold.

    The gift appears in the 3-D view in your character's hand.



To stop holding a gift in the 3-D view
  1. Click the My Things tab .

  2. From the My Things menu, click My Gifts.

  3. From the list that appears under My Gifts, click the gift you want to stop holding.

    The gift appears in the Web pane, and a list of options appears below the gift in the My Things menu.

  4. From the list of options, click Put Away.

    The gift is removed from your character's hand.
