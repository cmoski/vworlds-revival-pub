Choosing a Character

In Desktop World, you use a character to represent yourself. The first time you enter Desktop World, your character is given a default appearance depending on the gender you chose in the Log On to Desktop World box. Once you enter the world, you can personalize that appearance to suit your tastes. When you leave Desktop World, the changes you have made are saved automatically. So the next time you log on to Desktop World, your character will reflect your changes from the previous session.

You can change your character in the My Appearance area on the My Profile menu.

There are six basic characters to choose from, and each has a slightly different appearance and set of gestures that make it unique. You can also change the color of your character's hair and skin and clothing.

To choose a character
  1. Click the My Profile tab.

    The My Appearance page appears by default.

  2. Make a selection from the Character menu.

    The new character appears in the Preview area.

Note You may also change your appearance by right-clicking your character in the 3-D pane and clicking My Profile from the menu that appears.
