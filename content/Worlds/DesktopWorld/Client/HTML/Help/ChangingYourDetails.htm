Changing Your Details
You can personalize the information you provide to other members of Desktop World in the My Details area of the My Profile menu. The My Details area also displays any optional information you have supplied in the Log On to Desktop World box.

The Private check box next to each item indicates whether members of Desktop World who are not on your My Friends list can view your details. If an item is marked Private, only those members you have added to your Friends list can view this information. If the Private box is not checked, the information is available to all other members in their People menu.

To change your details
  1. Click the My Profile tab .

  2. In the My Profile menu, click My Details.

  3. Click inside the text box that you would like to change and type your information.

  4. To save the information you have added, click .

Note You can also change your details from the People menu.
