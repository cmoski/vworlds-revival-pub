Changing Your Look

In Desktop World, you can change your appearance by making selections in the My Appearance section of the My Profile menu. Changing your appearance includes selecting a character, choosing the colors for many of your character's features, and choosing a mood.


You have a choice of 6 characters to choose from. After selecting a character that you are comfortable with, you can customize your appearance by adjusting the colors of your clothing, skin, and hair. For each item, you can choose from a preset menu of four common colors, or from a palette of many other colors. The colors you select can be seen in the Preview area. When you find a color combination that you like, click to change the colors of your character and save your changes. Your color adjustments are saved between sessions in Desktop World.

Clicking resets your color selections to your most recent saved state (the last time you clicked ).

Note Changing your Character selection in the My Appearance area does not change the colors you have already chosen, so feel free to experiment with the way different color combinations look on each character. Other members of Desktop World cannot see your changes until you click .

To change the color of an item in your appearance
  1. Click the My Profile tab .

    The My Appearance section appears by default.

  2. From the list that appears below the Character box, click the item you would like to change.

    The color of the box around the item you clicked changes to blue.

  3. Click a preset color or select a color from the palette.

    Your selection appears in the Preview area.

  4. To save your selection, click .

Note You may also access the My Appearance area by right-clicking your character in the 3-D pane and clicking My Profile from the menu that appears. Your camera view must be set to facing-you.
