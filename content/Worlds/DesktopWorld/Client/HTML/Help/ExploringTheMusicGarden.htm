Exploring the Music Garden

The Music Garden is an area in Desktop World for you to explore and express yourself musically.

Many of the objects in the Music Garden make a sound when you click them. When you click a musical item, other members in the Music Garden can hear the sound it makes. You can click additional items, creating more sounds playing at the same time. Other members can also click musical objects to collaborate with you, adding to the sounds you've created.

Note When you enter the Music Garden, you will not hear the sounds that have been created before you entered the area. You will only hear the musical items that are clicked after you enter the area. Also, when you leave the garden, you will continue to hear the musical items that were clicked while you were in the garden, until those items are finished playing.

To play in the Music Garden
  1. Click the Destinations tab.

    The Destinations menu appears.

  2. On the Destinations menu, click Music Garden.

    Your character moves to the Music Garden.

  3. Click an item in the 3-D view, such as the basket.
