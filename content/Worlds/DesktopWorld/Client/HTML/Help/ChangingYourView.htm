Changing Your View

In Desktop World, you can move forward and backward and turn to the left and right to explore the world around you. You have a companion camera that silently flies alongside you, giving a viewpoint over your shoulder, through your eyes, or facing you.

To see how you look to others, click the camera angle button until you are facing yourself. It is a bit tricky to navigate Desktop World in this mode since you cannot see where you are going, but you can use this view to see your gestures and appearance.

There are three possible views, so if you don't like the new view, click the button again until you find the viewpoint you like best.

Note When viewing the 3-D area in over-the-shoulder mode, you will not be able to see the effect of clicking your Turn Around or Smile Gesture buttons.



To change your viewpoint
  • Click the camera angle button .
