Sending Gifts

In Desktop World, you can send personalized gifts to other members. The gift carts on the observation deck have a variety of virtual flowers or balloons that you can send to individual members. A card that you can fill in is also provided with each gift.

  

When you click a flower or balloon from a cart in the 3-D view, the item and an empty card appear in the Web pane. You can select up to six items from a cart, but you cannot combine flowers and balloons in the same gift. You can, however, send as many separate gifts as you like.

You can arrange items in a gift in any way you prefer. To arrange the items you have chosen, click an item in the Web pane and drag it with the mouse. To fill in the card, click in the text area and type your message. Clicking removes all of the flowers or balloons you have selected and deletes the contents of the card. To send your gift, select a recipient in the To box and click .

You can send a gift to any member in Desktop World whether or not that person is logged on. If the recipient is not logged on, the gift is saved for them until they enter Desktop World and view the gift. However, you cannot send a gift to a member who has placed you on his or her My Ignored list.



To send a gift to another member in Desktop World
  1. Click the Destinations tab .

    The Destinations menu appears.

  2. From the Destinations menu, click Gift Carts.

    Your character automatically navigates to the gift carts on the observation deck.

  3. Click a balloon or a flower on either of the carts.

    A gift with an blank card appears in the Web pane.

  4. In the To box, select the member to whom you would like to send the gift.

  5. Click as many as six items from the cart in the 3-D view and arrange them in the Web pane however you prefer. You can also type a message in the card by clicking inside the text area of the card and typing your message.

  6. To send your gift, click .

    A message appears in the Web pane alerting you that your gift has been sent. A message also appears in the recipient's chat history area that informs that member that he or she has received a new gift.
