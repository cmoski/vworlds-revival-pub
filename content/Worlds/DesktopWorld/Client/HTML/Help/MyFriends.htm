My Friends
The My Friends area displays the members of Desktop World that can see your private profile information. Members on your My Friends list have accepted your invitation to be added to this list. This list is saved between sessions in Desktop World.




My Friends options
Profile
Displays the profile information of other members of Desktop World. If the member you select has added you to their My Friends list, you can see items in their profile information that they have marked private.


Undo Friend
Removes a member from your My Friends list, preventing that member from viewing your private profile information. If you remove a member from this list, a message appears in that member's chat history area that says, "You have been removed from [your name]'s friend list." A similar message appears in your chat history area to confirm that you have removed that member from your friends list.


Ignore
Places a member on your My Ignored list, preventing that member from communicating with you. If you add a member to this list, a message appears in their chat history area that says, "[Your name] is ignoring you." A similar message appears in your chat history area to confirm that you are ignoring that member.


Whisper To
Sends a private message to a member. If you click the whisper option, a Whisper box appears with the member's name in the To box. Type your private message in the text box and click Send, or press ENTER.


Send Note
Sends a private, personalized note to a member on your My Friends list. If you click Send Note, a Note box appears with the name of the member in the To box. Type your note, select a stationery style, and click Send, or press ENTER.


Approach
Moves your character in the 3-D view to the same area that the selected member is currently in.
