Discussion Area

The Discussion Area is your place to express your ideas, ask questions and stimulate conversations with other people in Desktop World. A discussion is an original message and its replies. The original messages are listed in alphabetical order with replies appearing below it in chronological order from oldest to most recent replies. If there are numerous topics and replies, you will need to scroll to see them all.

Reading a Discussion Topic
  1. Click any discussion to open it. The original message appears at the top in a yellow box above the list of discussion topics and replies.
Starting a Discussion
  1. To start a discussion, click on New Topic. A yellow box with topic and comment boxes appears above list of discussion topics and replies.

  2. Type in a topic in the the Topic box.

  3. You can choose to use your username or be anonymous. The default selection is your username.

  4. Compose your message in the comments box.

  5. Click Save to post your message for others to read.

Replying to a Discussion Topic
  1. To add a reply, click a discussion on the list to open it.

  2. Click Reply located to the right of both the original message header and any reply headers.

  3. Compose your reply, and then click Save.

  4. Your reply will appear beneath the original message, at the bottom of any replies that were added prior to yours.
