Going to Sleep

In Desktop World, a character in the 3-D view can appear to be "sleeping." After 15 minutes of inactivity in the 3-D view and chat text box, your character's appearance changes to a resting pose, as if you are asleep. For example, if you are busy composing e-mail, surfing the World Wide Web, or away from your computer for a while, your character will appear to other members to be sleeping after 15 minutes. This lets others know you are still in Desktop World, but you are busy at the moment doing other things.

When you are in sleep mode, your name remains in the Who Is Here submenu of the People area. Other members can still send you gifts and notes, and you will continue to hear the audible alert notifying you when a member enters Desktop World. If you receive a gift or note, an exclamation point will appear on the My Things menu tab.



To "awaken" your character
Choose one of these options:
  • Type a message in the chat text box and press ENTER.

  • Click in the 3-D area and change your position.

  • Click one of the navigation buttons in the Destinations menu, such as Resource Desk.
