Game Area

The Game Room is your guide to playing games in Desktop World and on the web.  You can participate as a player or watch others play chess, and you can play solo games by yourself.  You can also access popular gaming web sites.

When you enter the game room, you will see large chess pieces on the floor.  To play chess, either click on the chess game text in the information pane, or click on a chess piece in the 3D view window.  This will open the chess board in the information pane.  Moving a piece on the 2D board, will move the piece in the 3D view.  Before beginning a game, make sure no one else is playing.  From the chess page, to go back to the main game room page, click on the Game Room text in the upper right hand corner, click on the back arrow, or click on the game room item in the list of destinations.

In Desktop World you can play the following solo games:  Connect Four, Hangman, or Swap Sides.  Click on the name of the game in the information pane to play the game.

Connect Four:  The object of the game is to connect four pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) before your “opponent.” The pieces will automatically drop to the bottom of column when you place a piece.

Hangman:  The object of Hangman is to guess the word before your “opponent” can create all the graphics to “hang the stickman.”  Click on a letter see whether or not it appears in the word.

Swap Sides:  The object of the Swap Sides is to move all the cakes and bears to the opposite side in 15 moves.

To access the game web sites, click on the link.

To play in the Game Room

1.       Click the Destinations tab.

2.       The Destinations menu appears. Game Room.

Your character moves to the Game Room and the chess pieces appear.

3.       Click a chess piece in the 3-D view, or click on a game name or link to game site to play.
