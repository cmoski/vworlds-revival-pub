Sending Notes

In Desktop World, you can send private, personalized notes to other members. You can send a note to individual members, or you can send the same note to everyone on your My Friends list at the same time. Four different designs are provided to customize your note.

You can send a note from either the Who Is Here or the Who Was Here submenu of the People menu. Click the name of another member from either of these lists, and the Send a Note option appears in the list of items below their name.

You can send a note to any member in Desktop World whether or not that person is logged on. If the recipient is not logged on, the note is saved for them until they enter Desktop World and choose to view the note. However, you cannot send a note to a member who has placed you on his or her My Ignored list.

Tip You can use a note as a personal reminder by sending one to yourself.



To send a note to another member in Desktop World
  1. Click the People tab .

    The People menu appears with the Who Is Here submenu open by default.

  2. From either the Who Is Here or the Who Was Here submenu, click the name of the member that you would like to receive your note.

  3. From the menu that appears, click Send a Note.

    A note, with the member's name in the To box, appears in the Web pane.

  4. From the Stationery list, you can select a type of stationery.

    If you choose a design other than Plain, the stationery you choose appears on the note.

  5. Type your note into the text area.

  6. To send your note, click .

    Tip You can also send a note to another member by right-clicking that person in the 3-D view and choosing Send a Note.


To send a note to all members on your My Friends list
  1. Click the My Profile tab .

  2. From the My Profile menu, click My Friends.

  3. From the list of options that appears below My Friends, click All My Friends.

    A note, with "All My Friends" in the To box, appears in the Web pane.

  4. Type your message in the text area of the note.

  5. Click .


To send a note to yourself
  1. Right-click your image in the 3-D area. To right-click your image, your camera view must be set to over-the-shoulder or facing you.

  2. From the menu that appears, click Send a Note.

  3. Make sure that your name is selected in the To list.

  4. Type your message in the note text area.

  5. Click .
