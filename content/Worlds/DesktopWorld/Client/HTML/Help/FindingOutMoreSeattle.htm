Finding Out More About Seattle

Seattle, home to the VWG.org team, provides a broad variety of activities and diversions for all interests and age groups. The city includes world-famous attractions such as Pike Place Market, the Space Needle, and Safeco Field. The greater Seattle area has many natural attractions as well, including hiking trails, beaches, and scenic bicycling routes.

In Desktop World, the Seattle Desk provides information on activities and resources available in the Seattle area. Each item on the desk and each poster on the walls surrounding the desk connects you to suggestions on things to do, places to visit, and assistance provisions available. The Seattle Desk receptionist, Morris, can supply a list of World Wide Web sites that describe further information on things to do and places to see in the Pacific Northwest.



To learn more about Seattle
  1. Click the Destinations tab .

  2. From the menu that appears, click Seattle Desk.

    In the 3-D view, your character automatically navigates to the Seattle Desk. The Seattle Desk Web page also appears in the Web pane.

  3. Click a poster or an item on the desk, such as the Space Needle, or an item from the Web page.

    Tip You can also find out more about Seattle by typing "Morris", the name of the Seattle Desk receptionist, in the chat text area and pressing ENTER.
