Accessing Your E-mail Account

Each member of Desktop World is can access their e-mail account with the Microsoft Network (MSN). This account, called a Hotmail account, is accessible both through the Desktop World window and external e-mail programs, such as Microsoft Outlook Express.

You can find instructions on using your e-mail account by visiting the MSN Hotmail World Wide Web site. To access this Web site, click the My Things tab, and then choose My E-mail from the list of options on the My Things menu.

To access your Hotmail account
  1. Click the My Things tab. The My Things menu appears.
  2. On the My Things menu, click My E-mail.

    The MSN Hotmail Web page appears in the Web pane.

  3. Follow the instructions on the Hotmail Web page.
