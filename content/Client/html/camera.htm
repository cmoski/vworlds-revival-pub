Choose a camera view relative to your
    avatar or attach to another object.




Through my eyes:
You view the world through your avatar's eyes. This is similar to your natural point of view.
In front of me:
You view yourself as others see you, with the camera staying in front of you as you move around.
Over my shoulder:
You view the world and the back of your own avatar's head and shoulders, as if standing behind your avatar.
You view the world from the object selected. If this object is moving, your point of view also moves.
