Room Portal
Room portals are doorways between rooms. These objects can transport avatars between the current room and another room in the same world. Portals go in one direction to another room.

You can link to the portal in the destination room. You can set the entry point (location of appearing avatars in this room) and the facing direction (which way avatars face on arrival in this room).

The default appearance of the portal is an open doorway with a blue sky and a desert setting.
