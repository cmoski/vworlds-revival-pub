World Management

The World Management lessons offer a more detailed look at scripts that interact with multiple users and custom data.
a We look at how to manage data for Virtual Worlds in script, including the use of property lists and maps. You can use these objects to put custom data into the world, so those objects can access that data and make use of it in their behavior.
a We also show the dynamic creation and destruction of objects from scripts; this enables a world to alter itself automatically.
